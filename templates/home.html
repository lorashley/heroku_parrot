Hello, Parrot Heroku Bot Owner!

You've successfully deployed an instance of this Webex Teams bot.

Reminder: In order for the bot to actually work, you need to create a webhook using your bot's authentication token.
The targetUrl will be the url of your Heroku app, it'll be something like appname.herokuapp.com, which will connect the client messages to the bot to the bot's backend on Heroku.

Your webhook request will look like a modified version of the following:
{
"name": "Heroku Parrot",
"targetUrl": "yourappname.herokuapp.com",
"resource": "messages",
"event": "created"
}
